Student dashboard

Project title, stage progress, activity calendar, document requirements, notifications, and complaint access.

Core Capabilities

Stage-Based Workflow

Concept, Proposal, and Thesis Submission stages with supervisor approval required before advancement.

Mandatory Uploads

Required documents such as Minutes of Presentation, Fee Statement, Transcript, Proposal Document, and Thesis Document are enforced per stage.

Notifications

Automated alerts and reminders for deadlines, document uploads, and complaint responses.

Role-Based Access

Distinct access for Students, Supervisors, Coordinators, and Senior Administrators with tailored dashboards and reports.

Who uses the system?

Students

Complete profile setup, select preferred supervisor, upload documents, track progress, and submit complaints confidentially.

Supervisors

Approve stage completion, define activities, review submissions, and monitor student progress.

Coordinators

Assign supervisors, monitor departmental progress, handle complaints, and generate student reports.
